# Public Visual System: Black Ledger Glass

## Art direction

- dark flagship hero
- sharp glass and console panels
- no blurry or stretched imagery
- clear CTA hierarchy
- readable stats on desktop and phone

## Non-goals

- generic light SaaS cards
- wallpaper-style blurry backgrounds
- text over busy images
- roadmap copy crowding the first fold

## Responsive floor

- 390x844
- 412x915
- 768x1024
- 1366x768
- 1440x900
- 1920x1080
