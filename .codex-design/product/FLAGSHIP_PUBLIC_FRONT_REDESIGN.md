# Flagship Public Front Redesign

## Goal

Make `chummer.run` feel like a first-party flagship entrance instead of a generic software homepage.

## Canonical sections

1. Hero: `Build the runner. Run the table. Keep the ledger honest.`
2. Five gateways: Build, Play, Packages, Karma Forge, Black Ledger
3. Governed Black Ledger stat preview
4. Karma Forge pipeline
5. Proof and account continuity rail
6. Participation preview and trust footer

## Route ownership

- Homepage CTA for Black Ledger points to `/ledger`
- Homepage CTA for Karma Forge points to `/karma-forge`
- Roadmap routes remain secondary explainers, not primary hubs

## Copy rules

- Product first, proof visible, no roadmap-overclaim
- Short sentences over dense explanatory rails
- No provider or operator naming on public surfaces
